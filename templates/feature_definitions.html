Dictionary of Terms 🌾🧺

Mean Temp (°C): Average temperature over a specified month and year.

Temp Range (°C): The difference between maximum temperature and minimum temperature for a specified month.

Heat Deg Days (°C): Heating Degree Day values averaged over a specified month and year. Heating Degree Day is a measurement that quantifies the demand for energy needed to heat a building.

Total Precip (mm): Total precipitation that occurs over a specified month and year.

Growing Degree Days: The accumulation of average daily temperatures. The measure of heat accumulation is used to predict plant development rates.
