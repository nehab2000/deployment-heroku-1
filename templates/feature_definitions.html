Dictionary of Terms 🌾🧺

Avg Mean Temp (°C): Daily mean temperature values averaged over a specified month and year.

Avg Temp Range (°C): The difference between average maximum temperature and average minimum temperature for a specified month.

Avg Heat Deg Days (°C): Heating Degree Day values averaged over a specified month and year. Heating Degree Day is a measurement that quantifies the demand for energy needed to heat a building.

Total Precip (mm): Total precipitation that occurs over a specified month and year.

Growing Degree Days: The accumulation of average daily temperatures. The measure of heat accumulation is used to predict plant development rates.
